Dumbdown is a next generation markup language that was inspired by Markdown, and emphasizes simplicity and readablity.

Introduction

The Dumbdown team designed Dumbdown to be so simple and straightforward that most users don't even need any training before they can start writing in Dumbdown!

FAQ

How do you make lists?

1. Just type a number!
2. Then type a period!
3. Voila, a list!

How do you insert a link?

Just take the link you want to insert (e.g. http://www.reddit.com/) and type it out!
e.g. http://www.reddit.com/

How do I quote something?

If you look carefully at your keyboard you might find a key that looks like this ".
This pair of levitating commas is actually called a quotation mark, and you can use them to signify quotes!

"Look, I'm a quote!".
